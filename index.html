Redactr

What is Redactr

Redactr is a tool to help safely share content on social media by scrambling portions of text you dont wan't the world or readers to see.

How to Redact

⊫ Write/Paste the content/words you want to redact in the textarea field.

⊫ Enter the words you need to be scrambled in the input field seperated by space(s).

⊫ Enter the symbol to be used as a replacement for the words to be scrambled. Eg Asterisk(*), question mark(?), dashes(-), underscore(_) etc.

⊫ Hit the Redact Now button to Redact the content.
